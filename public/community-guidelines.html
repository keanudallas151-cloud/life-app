Community Guidelines

These guidelines apply to all posts, comments, and interactions in Life. community spaces.

1. Respect First

  • No harassment, intimidation, hate speech, or personal attacks.
  • Critique ideas, not people.
  • No doxxing or sharing private personal information.

2. Keep It Useful

  • Stay on-topic and avoid low-value spam.
  • Mark opinion as opinion; avoid presenting guesses as facts.
  • No repetitive self-promotion or referral flooding.

3. Safety And Legality

  • No illegal content, fraud guidance, or explicit content.
  • No impersonation of people, businesses, or Life. staff.
  • No attempts to exploit platform vulnerabilities.

4. Enforcement

Content may be removed and accounts may be warned, suspended, or terminated for guideline violations. Serious violations can result in immediate account termination.

Report issues: legal@lifeapp.com.au
